About Me

I am a student currently studying Information Technology and Graphic Design. I graduated from a high school early college program after taking community college courses for several years before my freshman year of college. I am also an artist, and I have been learning to create digital art using software such as Photoshop, Illustractor, and Clip Studio Paint.

I am interested in web design, graphic design, and video game concept art. I hope to eventually start my own business to work as a freelancer designing websites, logos and icons, as well as creating enviornment, costume and character concept art for video games. For more information about me, visit my resume and my fun facts pages.
